Target Audience

Our overall goal with this website is to support businesses, new startup businesses, and to attract more outside businesses to the Pinnacle City area. With this in mind, we will outline two personas and 5 scenarios that will represent our target audience.

Personas

Eduardo Messino

  • 30 years old
  • Single Male, engaged to be married
  • Masters Degree, Software Engineering
  • 5 years experience in the tech field
  • Plans to expand his established social media startup
  • Has looked at various community chambers of commerce websites
  • Primarily uses a laptop for browsing, but also uses a cell phone
  • Needs a community rich in resources in order to build his business
  • Looking to move to a family community with many outdoor amenities
  • Needs educated technicians to hire

    • Elizabeth Hoffman

  • 56 years old
  • Divorced female, 3 grown children
  • No formal education
  • Life-long resident of Pinnacle City
  • Worked at a garden nursery for 15 years
  • Wants to start her own nursery business
  • Needs help with marketing, networking
  • Has asked friends and family in business and was referred to Pinnacle City's website
  • Primarily uses a desktop computer, but occasionally uses her phone
  • Wants to stay local because she has children and grandchildren here
  • Needs help finding a locale to rent for her business
  • Needs community resources and support
  • Needs networking to find a few young people to help her
